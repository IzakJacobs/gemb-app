1. System Overview

The MBGE Access Control System is a web-based Progressive Web App (PWA) that manages visitor passes, service provider permits, vehicle records, gate access, and estate notices for Mossel Bay Golf Estate.

The system is accessed at https://mbge.ink/AS-menu.html from any modern browser — no app installation is required, though residents may add it to their home screen for quick access.

What's New in v2.1

Proximity Gate Unlock Residents can now open any estate gate directly from their phone, without presenting a tag or triggering LPR/facial recognition. GPS is used to confirm the resident is within 100 m of the selected gate. Every unlock is logged automatically.
Visitor GPS Navigation Visitor passes now include a "Get Directions" section. Visitors tap one button to open Google Maps, Waze, or Apple Maps pre-loaded with the resident's home address — no manual address entry needed.
WhatsApp OTP Login Residents receive one-time login codes via WhatsApp, reducing forgotten-PIN lockouts.

4. Resident Portal

The resident portal gives homeowners and registered occupants full self-service access to visitor management, gate control, vehicle records, and helpdesk tickets.

4.1 Logging In

  • Open https://mbge.ink/AS-menu.html and tap Resident Login.
  • Enter your Erf number (e.g. ERF123). A dropdown of registered occupants for that erf will appear.
  • Select your name from the dropdown and enter your 6-digit PIN.
  • On a new device you will receive a WhatsApp OTP on your registered number. Enter it to verify the device.
  • Verified devices are remembered for up to 10 years via a secure cookie — you will not be prompted for OTP again on the same device unless cookies are cleared.
Forgotten PIN? Tap Reset PIN on the login screen. A reset link is sent to your registered mobile number via WhatsApp.

4.2 Managing Visitors

From the resident menu, tap Visitors to invite personal guests.

Adding a Visitor

  • Tap Add Visitor.
  • Enter the visitor's full name, ID number or passport, and vehicle registration plate (optional).
  • Set the valid-from and valid-to dates. A visitor pass is only active within this window.
  • Optionally enter the visitor's mobile number to send them the pass directly.
  • Tap Save. A unique 3XXXXX code and QR pass are generated automatically.
  • Share the pass link with your visitor (WhatsApp, SMS, or copy the link).

Visitor Pass Page

When the visitor opens the pass link on their phone they see:

  • Status badge — Valid Pass, Not Yet Active, or Expired
  • Their name, valid dates, and resident address
  • A QR code to present at the gate
  • The Get Directions section (see §4.5 below)

Deleting / Expiring a Visitor

Tap the red Delete button next to a visitor's name in the list. This immediately invalidates their pass.

4.3 Visitor GPS Navigation NEW v2.1

Visitor passes now include a Get Directions section below the QR code, showing your home address and three one-tap navigation buttons.

How it works The resident's address is pulled from the database when the pass is generated. Visitors do not need to know the address — they just tap the app they prefer.
  • Google Maps — opens the Google Maps app or website
  • Waze — opens Waze with turn-by-turn directions
  • Apple Maps — opens Apple Maps (best on iPhone)
Resident tip: Make sure your address is correct in the system. Contact the admin if it needs updating.

4.5 Proximity Gate Unlock NEW v2.1

Residents can open any estate gate from their phone without a tag or access card, as long as they are physically near the gate.

Step-by-step

  • Log in to the Resident portal and tap the green Open Gate card on the main menu.
  • Choose your entrance — Schoeman Street or Church Street.
  • Tap the specific gate you want to open (e.g. SS Gate 1).
  • When prompted, allow the browser to access your location. This is required for the distance check and is not stored beyond the unlock log.
  • The system measures the distance between your phone and the gate using GPS (Haversine formula).
  • Within 100 m — a large green "Push Button to Open Gate" button appears. Tap it once.
  • Beyond 100 m — a yellow warning shows your actual distance. Move closer to the gate and try again.
Important
  • Location permission must be granted in your browser. If denied, go to your browser's site settings and allow location for mbge.ink.
  • Every gate unlock is logged with your name, the gate, distance, and timestamp. This log is visible to Security and Admin.
  • The 100 m limit is enforced server-side — it cannot be bypassed by the user interface.

Recent Unlocks

The bottom of the Open Gate page shows your 5 most recent gate unlocks, including the gate name, distance, and time.

4.6 Vehicles

From the resident menu tap Vehicles to register or remove vehicles associated with your property. Registered plates are recognised by LPR cameras at the gates and do not require a manual guard check on entry.

4.7 Helpdesk

Tap Helpdesk from the resident menu to log a query or complaint. Security and Admin staff can view and respond to tickets. You will receive a reference number for follow-up.

5. Guard Portal

Access at https://mbge.ink/guard.php. Guards log in with their assigned guard credentials (not a resident PIN).

Gate Verification

  • Select Verify Access from the guard menu.
  • Scan the visitor's QR code using the camera button, OR type the 6-digit code manually (3XXXXX for visitors, 7XXXXX for service providers).
  • The system displays the pass status instantly:
    • ✓ Valid — name, photo (if any), vehicle plate, and valid dates shown in green
    • ⚠ Not Active Yet — pass start date is in the future
    • ⛔ Expired — pass end date has passed; deny entry
    • ⛔ Not Found — code does not exist; deny entry and alert Security
  • Tap Log Entry to record the visit in the access log.

Access Log

Guards can view the current day's access log showing all entries and exits recorded at their gate.

Panic Alert

In an emergency, guards access the panic function from https://mbge.ink/panic/panic_login.php. See §8 for full details.

PIN / Password Reset

Guards cannot reset their own credentials. Contact the Security office or Admin.

6. Security Officer Portal

Access at https://mbge.ink/security.php. Security officers have an elevated view of the estate without full admin rights.

Service Provider Approvals

Delivery and contractor passes flagged for approval appear in the Approvals queue. Review the request details and tap Approve or Reject.

Access Logs

Full searchable log of all gate events — entries, exits, QR scans, and proximity unlocks.

Guard Management

Add, edit, or deactivate guard accounts from the Guard Management section.

QR Lookup

Enter any 6-digit code to view the full details and status of a visitor or service provider pass.

7. Admin Portal

Access at https://mbge.ink/admin.php. Admin has unrestricted access to all system functions.

Security reminder The default admin password is Admin@MBGE2026. Change it immediately after first login. Do not share admin credentials.

Resident Management

Add, edit, or delete resident accounts via Residents Admin. Each resident record includes:

  • Erf number, occupant code, resident name
  • Address (used for visitor GPS navigation)
  • Phone number (for WhatsApp OTP)
  • Occupant type (owner, tenant, etc.) and status (active / inactive)

Newsletter & Notice Upload

Upload PDF or image files via Newsletters Admin. Uploaded files are stored in the /uploads/ folder and listed in the resident notice board.

Data Exports

From the Export menu download CSV files of visitors, service providers, vehicles, and access logs. An activity report summarising gate events is also available.

Helpdesk

View and respond to resident helpdesk tickets from the Admin Helpdesk menu.

Nightly Cleanup

A cron job runs at 02:00 every night to purge data older than the POPIA retention period (default 90 days). The schedule in cPanel is:

0 2 * * * /usr/bin/php /home/USERNAME/public_html/cron_cleanup.php

8. Panic Alert

The panic alert system allows guards to send an immediate emergency notification to the Security Control Room.

  • Open https://mbge.ink/panic/panic_login.php — a dedicated bookmark/shortcut should be on every guard device.
  • Log in with guard credentials.
  • On the confirmation screen, tap Send Panic Alert.
  • The alert is dispatched and logged with the guard's name, location, and timestamp.
Test mode Do not trigger a panic alert for testing purposes during operational hours. Coordinate test times with the Security office.

9. POPIA Compliance

The system is designed to comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

  • Data retention: Personal data is automatically purged after 90 days (configurable by Admin in the settings table).
  • Biometrics: No biometric or facial recognition data is collected or stored.
  • Criminal records: No criminal record checks are performed or stored (unconstitutional).
  • POPIA notices: All data-capture forms display a POPIA information notice before submission.
  • Proximity log: The gate proximity unlock log retains only resident ID, gate name, distance, and timestamp — no precise location trajectory is stored.
  • Minimum data: Only the data necessary for estate security is collected.

10. Troubleshooting

Proximity Gate Unlock — common issues

ProblemSolution
"Location permission denied" Go to browser site settings for mbge.ink and set Location to Allow. On iOS use Safari → Settings → Safari → Location.
Distance shown is wrong (e.g. 2000 m when at the gate) Ensure you are outdoors with a clear sky view. Indoor or underground GPS is inaccurate. Refresh and try again.
"Too Far to Open Gate" despite being at the gate GPS can drift up to 50 m in some conditions. Wait 10 seconds for the position to stabilise and tap the gate card again.
Button appears but gate does not open The hardware trigger may not be connected. Contact the estate manager — gate hardware integration is in progress.

Login issues

ProblemSolution
Erf number not recognised Check you are using the correct erf format (e.g. ERF123 not 123). Contact Admin if not resolved.
WhatsApp OTP not received Check WhatsApp is installed and your number is correct. Allow 2 minutes. Request a resend or contact Admin.
PIN locked out Use the Reset PIN link on the login screen or contact Admin.

Visitor pass not accepted at gate

  • Confirm the pass dates include today (check the visitor list in your resident portal).
  • Ensure the visitor is showing the correct link — it must start with https://mbge.ink/visitor_qr.php?code=3…
  • If the QR code cannot be scanned, ask the guard to type the 6-digit code manually.
